Who am I?

I'm Jai Prakash, a medical student with a passion for technology and web development.

I'm passionate about creating digital solutions. My journey began with building simple static pages and evolved into developing full-stack applications.

I specialize in crafting responsive websites and configuring server environments. What started as curiosity has grown into creating functional web systems that solve real problems.

When I'm not studying medicine, I enjoy coding, exploring new technologies and mentoring beginners in web development.

I'm currently pursuing my MD (MBBS) degree at Georgian National University (SEU), where I'm deepening my knowledge of medical science and patient care.

My medical education complements my technical skills, allowing me to approach healthcare challenges with a unique perspective that combines medical knowledge with technological solutions.

I'm particularly interested in how technology can enhance healthcare delivery, medical education, and patient outcomes. This dual expertise positions me to potentially bridge the gap between medicine and technology in innovative ways.

When I'm not in lectures or clinical rotations, I continue to work on web development projects that often intersect with medical applications.
